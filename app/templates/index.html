Welcome to Kitchen Contractor!

Kitchen Contractor gives you the tools to be your own general contractor for your kitchen renovation.

Use the options at the top of the page to plan a new project or continue an existing one

FAQ:

What is KitchenContractor?

KitchenContractor is a platform that allows you to manage your own kitchen renovation

Why should I use Kitchen Contractor?
  • The average general contractor takes about 10-20% of the cost of construction as a fee - money which can be invested into your kitchen instead. Additionally, as your own general contractor, you control exactly what the final version of your kitchen will look like.
    • How does it work?

    Home Owners:

  • Meet with an architect/designer to plan what your renovated kitchen will look like. You can search for them Here
  • Start a new project using the link at the top of the page, and fill out the questionnaire
  • You now have a step by step plan for your kitchen renovation, which you can find under your profile
  • Search for contractors that the plan calls for
  • Look through contractor past projects and reviews to find the contractor right for you
  • Contact the contractor(s) and request an estimate for the job
  • Send a join request to the contractor whose offer you accept
  • repeat the process for each step of the plan
  • After all contractors have completed their roles and your kitchen has been renovated, mark the project as completed
  • Don't forget to leave a review for the contractors you hired!

    • Contractors:

  • Set up your profile page
  • If a homeowner contacts you, give them an estimate for the project
  • If the homeowner accepts, you will receive a request to join the project
  • Do your part of the Renovation!

    • Both types of users can manage their current projects and pending requests on their profiles
